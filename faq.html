What is Salvium Protocol?
Salvium Protocol is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform that aims to provide private, secure, and efficient financial services. It combines cutting-edge privacy technology with DeFi functionality to create a comprehensive ecosystem for users who value their privacy and security.
How does Salvium ensure privacy?
Salvium Protocol utilizes advanced cryptographic techniques and zero-knowledge proofs to ensure transaction privacy. Every transaction on the platform is encrypted and untraceable, while still maintaining the ability to verify its validity. This approach provides users with complete financial privacy without compromising on security or compliance.
What are SAL tokens used for?
SAL tokens are the native utility tokens of the Salvium Protocol ecosystem. They serve multiple purposes: governance voting, staking rewards, accessing premium features, and facilitating private transactions within the network. Token holders can actively participate in protocol decisions and earn rewards for contributing to the network's security and growth.
