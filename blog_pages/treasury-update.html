Treasury Update

January 5, 2024 4 min read

We are excited to announce that the Salvium Treasury has successfully locked 50% of its funds for a period of 2 years, demonstrating our long-term commitment to the project and our confidence in its future. This strategic decision aligns with our vision of building a sustainable and trustworthy cryptocurrency ecosystem.

Treasury Lock Details

  • 50% of Treasury funds locked
  • Lock period: 2 years (until August 2026)
  • Transparent and verifiable on-chain
  • Demonstrates long-term commitment

Why Lock Treasury Funds?

This decision serves multiple purposes:

  1. Shows our commitment to long-term development
  2. Provides stability to the Salvium ecosystem
  3. Protects against market volatility
  4. Builds trust with our community

Impact on Development

The remaining unlocked funds will continue to support:

  • Ongoing development initiatives
  • Marketing and community growth
  • Security audits and improvements
  • Operational expenses

Future Plans

Looking ahead, we will:

  • Continue transparent treasury management
  • Regular updates on fund utilization
  • Community involvement in major decisions
  • Focus on sustainable growth

This treasury lock represents another step in our commitment to building a strong, sustainable, and trustworthy privacy-focused cryptocurrency. We thank our community for their continued support and trust in the Salvium project.
